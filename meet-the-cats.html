Persian Cats

Appearance: Known for their long, flowing fur and flat faces. Their coat comes in various colors, including white, black, and blue.

Personality: Persians are gentle and affectionate. They enjoy a calm environment and are perfect for indoor living.

Siamese Cats

Appearance: Sleek and slender with striking blue almond-shaped eyes and a short coat that comes in points (ears, face, paws, and tail) of a darker color.

Personality: Siamese cats are known for their vocal and social nature. They are very interactive and enjoy companionship.

Care: Minimal grooming needed. Regular play and mental stimulation are crucial for their well-being.

Maine Coons

Appearance: Large and muscular with a long, shaggy coat. They have tufted ears and bushy tails.

Personality: Maine Coons are friendly and easygoing. They are good with children and other pets.

Care: Requires regular brushing due to their long fur. They also need space to roam and exercise.

Scottish Folds

Appearance: Distinctive for their folded ears that give them an owl-like look. They have a round face and a medium-sized body.

Personality: Scottish Folds are affectionate and calm. They adapt well to different living situations and are great lap cats.

Care: Minimal grooming required. Regular vet check-ups are important to monitor ear health.
